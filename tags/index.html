Pineapple Curry

Difficulty: easy Time: 50 mins Ingredients: Coconut oil Shallots Garlic Thai red curry paste turmeric ginger Sweet potatoes Carrots Peppers Coconut milk Soy sauce Pineapple Kale Tofu Coriander Lemon Peanuts Chickpeas Quinoa Instructions Start with a huge pot, put some coconut oil in and add the garlic/shallots (a lot) until they’re starting to fry. Add the turmeric/ginger/red curry paste, mix and so on. Add the potatoes/carrots/peppers and mix so that the spices are spread out properly, give it a bit of time for the flavour to absorb.
