Mango Salad

Difficulty: very easy Time: 15 minutes Ingredients (6 people): 4 Mangoes 2 Cucumbers 2 Red peppers 5x400g Canned Chickpeas Coriander Seasoning: 1 Lime White Wine Vinegar (or similar) Hemp oil, Olive oil (or similar) Salt Chili (powdered or chopped) Instructions: Peel and cut the mangoes and cucumbers into small pieces. Cut the red peppers into small pieces. Add the seasoning and chopped coriander, mix everything.

Quinoa Salad

Difficulty: very easy Time: 25 mins Ingredients (6 persons): 500g quinoa 80g kale 4 apples 300g sweet peppers 5 spring onions 240g can chickpeas 150g cranberries walnut to taste Seasoning: 1 lemon Mustard (1 full tsp) White Vinegar (2 tsp) Balsamic Vinegar (1 tsp) Hemp oil (2 tsp) Instructions: Cook the quinoa in vegetable stock. Wash and cut the sweet peppers, apples, spring onions and kale in small pieces.
