Halloumi Paprika

September 4, 2019

Difficulty: very easy

Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients (6 people):

  • Halloumi (225g)

Seasoning:

  • Paprika (more than one type if available)
  • Thyme
  • Pepper
  • Cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

Cut the halloumi into small slices. In a big pan heat some olive oil, then place the halloumi slices in the pan, add pepper and paprika. Fry until golden on one side then turn and add more seasoning. Add thyme to finish with.
